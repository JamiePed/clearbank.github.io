---
title: "Bacs Direct Credit Inbound Payment Created webhook"
version: 2
webhook: true
---

This webhook confirms that a Bacs Direct Credit inbound payment has been received for the Processing date (Day 2) specified.

A business banking with another bank has sent a credit payment to a
ClearBank account. With this webhook, ClearBank notifies that the credit
has been received. You can then use this information
to notify the intended recipients that there will be a credit to their
account on the working day following the processing day (specified in the ProcessingDate field), subject to the credit not being recalled
before 4pm on the same day.


#### Request body

```json
{
    "Type": "BacsDirectCreditInboundPaymentCreatedV2",
    "Version": 2,
    "Payload": {...},
    "Nonce":
}
```
